# Incident Escalation Procedure

## Overview

Defines the enterprise escalation process for IT incidents based on severity and business impact.

---

## Escalation Matrix

| Severity | Priority | Escalation |
|----------|----------|------------|
| Critical | P1 | Platform Engineering + Incident Manager |
| High | P2 | Senior Support Engineer |
| Medium | P3 | Support Team |
| Low | P4 | Service Desk |

---

## Response Targets

- P1 – 15 minutes
- P2 – 30 minutes
- P3 – 2 hours
- P4 – Next Business Day

---

## Notification

Notify all relevant stakeholders immediately for Critical incidents.
